About

Traditional Yoga is a sacred transmission — not learned, but lived — through the timeless bond of Guru and Shishya. In this ancient lineage, the Guru doesn’t just teach techniques but awakens inner wisdom, guiding the Shishya beyond posture to self-realization.

At THE GURUKUL ESSENCE, we revive this living tradition where Yoga is not a class — but a journey, a surrender, and a transformation through presence, practice, and grace.
